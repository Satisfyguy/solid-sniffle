What is non-custodial?

Unlike centralized exchanges, this marketplace NEVER holds your private keys or controls your funds. You run your own Monero wallet RPC, and only you can sign transactions.

Your wallet RPC must be running on localhost only (127.0.0.1) for security reasons.
Choose "Buyer" if you want to purchase items, or "Vendor" if you want to sell.
